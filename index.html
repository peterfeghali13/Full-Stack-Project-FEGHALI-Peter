About GTA VI

Grand Theft Auto VI is the upcoming open-world action-adventure game by Rockstar Games. Set in the fictional state of Leonida — inspired by Florida — the game returns players to the iconic neon-lit streets of Vice City for the first time since 2006.

For the first time in the mainline series, players take on the role of a female protagonist: Lucia, alongside her partner Jason. Their story of survival and ambition plays out across a sprawling open world filled with secrets, heists, and chaos at every turn.

GTA VI is officially confirmed for release on November 19, 2026 on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. A PC version will follow at a later date.
